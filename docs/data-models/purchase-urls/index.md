## Model Properties

> ### cardKingdom <i class="optional"></i>
>
> The URL to purchase a product on [Card Kingdom](https://www.cardkingdom.com?partner=mtgjson&utm_source=mtgjson&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=mtgjson).
>
> - **Type:** `string`
> - **Introduced:** `v5.0.0`
